Conference Venue


The CCSC 2026 will take place at the Bayerische Akademie der Wissenschaften (BAdW) located in the heart of Munich, Germany.

The Bayerische Akademie der Wissenschaften, founded in 1759, is one of the oldest and most prestigious academic institutions in Germany. Situated in the historic Munich Residenz, it offers an inspiring setting for scientific exchange and collaboration.

Munich is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city renowned for its strong scientific, technological, and industrial landscape. It is home to several world-leading institutions, including the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität (LMU), the Technical University of Munich (TUM), and numerous Max Planck and Fraunhofer Institutes. With a rich cultural heritage and dynamic research environment, Munich offers an ideal backdrop for the CCSC 2026 — connecting tradition with cutting-edge science.




Travel


The conference will be held at the Bayerische Akademie der Wissenschaften (BAdW) in the Munich Residenz.

The closest airports are Munich Int. Airport (MUC) and Frankfurt Int. Airport (FRA). There are numerous buses that lead to the conference venue, i.e. via Munich central station. From MUC, you can take tram S1 or S8 to reach Munich central station. From FRA, there are regular train connections with a total travel time of approximately 3.5 hours to reach Munich central station. From Munich central station you can take U4 (direction: Arabellapark) or U5 (direction: Neuperlach Süd). Ideal drop-off point is Odeonsplatz. The venue is located at Alfons-Goppel-Straße 11.

For timetables and to book your train ticket please consult Deutsche Bahn (for the train) and MVG (for public transportation).
