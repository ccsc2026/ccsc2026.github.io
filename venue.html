Conference Venue


The CCSC 2026 will take place at the Department of Chemistry, at the FAU, Erlangen, Germany.

Erlangen is a popular small city especially amongst young people, not only culturally but also because it provides numerous career opportunities in academic and industrial research or development. It's home to the headquarter of Siemens Healthineers, a Max-Planck and a Frauenhofer Institue as well as the FAU, which many renowned scientists hail from, such as Emmy Noether, Hermann E. Fischer, Justus von Liebig, Georg S. Ohm and many others. Today, it is a city known for its history of great scientific and medical achievements and a promising future filled with many more breakthroughs.




Travel


The closest airports are Frankfurt International Airport (FRA) and Nuremberg Airport (NUE). From FRA there are regular train connections to Erlangen, with a transfer at the Nuremberg Central Station (average travel duration is about 2.5h). For timetables and to book your train ticket please consult Deutsche Bahn
